FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pet transportation services. Still have questions? Contact us anytime.

Pricing is based on distance, size and breed of your pet, service type, number of pets, and special care requirements. Every quote is personalized — fill out our free quote form or call us for an exact price. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

We drive approximately 500–600 miles per day, making regular stops for rest, water, and bathroom breaks. A transport from Houston to New York (approximately 1,600 miles) typically takes 3–4 days. We'll give you an exact timeline during your consultation.

Our vehicles are climate-controlled and purpose-equipped for pet transport. Pets are secured with appropriate crates or harnesses. We make regular stops for water, feeding, and rest. Our drivers are trained in animal behavior and basic pet first aid. All transports include GPS tracking and real-time updates.

Every transport is covered by our comprehensive pet transport liability insurance, included in your transport fee at no additional cost. For specific questions about coverage limits and exclusions, please contact us directly during your consultation.

Our flight nanny travels in-cabin with your pet — never in cargo. We coordinate the flight booking, ensure an FAA-approved carrier is used, handle check-in procedures, and ensure your pet is comfortable throughout the flight. This is the safest way for your pet to fly.

Yes! We can transport multiple pets from the same household together. Since we offer VIP solo transports, your pets will never be mixed with pets from other families. Please mention the number and types of pets when requesting your quote.

We require current vaccination records and a health certificate from a licensed veterinarian (especially for interstate transport). Some states have additional requirements — we'll walk you through exactly what's needed for your specific route.

Cancellations made 7 or more days before the scheduled transport receive a full refund of any deposit. We will NEVER cancel on you — our Never Late, Never Cancel guarantee means we honor every commitment we make.
