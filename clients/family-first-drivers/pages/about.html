Our Story

A Specialized Animal Logistics Company Built on Trust

Family First Drivers is a specialized animal transportation and logistics company based in Houston, Texas. We provide long-distance pet transport services across the United States — with a focus on safety, reliability, and premium care.

We started with a simple observation: most pet transport companies treat animals like packages. Multiple animals jammed into one vehicle, owners with no updates, and a race-to-the-bottom on price at the expense of care.

We built something different. With a growing network of professional drivers and hundreds of successful transports completed, Family First Drivers continues to expand while maintaining our commitment to VIP-level service — one family at a time.
