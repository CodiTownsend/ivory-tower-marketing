Long Distance Transport FAQ

Transit time depends on the route. We drive your pet directly to the destination without unnecessary detours. We stop every 3–4 hours for the pet's needs. A Houston to Chicago route typically takes 2–3 days. Contact us for a specific timeline estimate on your route.

Just provide your pet's regular food — we bring everything else (crate, water, treats, bowls, potty pads). Current vaccination records are recommended. Make sure your pet's ID tags and microchip info are current. We'll walk you through everything at booking.

Your pet is NEVER in the vehicle with other families' animals. VIP solo transport means one family at a time, every time. This eliminates disease risk and aggressive interaction concerns entirely.
