What Is a Flight Nanny?

A Dedicated Human Companion for Your Pet's Entire Flight

A flight nanny is a trained, experienced animal handler who accompanies your pet on a commercial flight — in the passenger cabin, under the seat, the entire journey.

This means your pet is never placed in cargo. They're monitored, comforted, and cared for every single minute of the flight. Our flight nannies are experienced with airline protocols, breed-specific needs, and in-flight calming techniques.

From the moment we pick up your pet in Houston to the moment we place them in your arms at the destination — your pet is never out of our sight.

⚠️ Why Cargo Is Risky

The USDA reports thousands of pet incidents in airline cargo each year — including injuries, illness, and death. In-cabin travel eliminates these risks entirely. Your pet deserves better than a baggage compartment.

The Process

How Our Flight Nanny Service Works

Every detail is handled for you — from documentation to delivery. Here's the complete step-by-step process.

1
Request & Consultation

Contact us with your pet's details, origin, destination, and preferred dates. We'll discuss your pet's specific needs, breed requirements, and confirm eligibility for in-cabin travel (weight/size limits apply per airline).

2
Documentation & Vet Coordination

We guide you through required documentation including a health certificate (required within 10 days of travel), proof of vaccinations, and any state-specific entry requirements for the destination. We coordinate directly with your vet.

3
Flight Booking & Carrier Setup

We identify the best flight routing for your pet, coordinate the airline pet reservation, and ensure an FAA-approved, airline-compliant soft-sided carrier is ready. We handle all the airline communication so you don't have to.

4
Pickup & Airport Check-In

Your flight nanny picks up your pet from your door in Houston, transports them to the airport, handles check-in, security screening, and gets your pet comfortably settled in the carrier at the gate.

5
In-Flight Care & Updates

Your pet rides under the seat in front of the flight nanny — monitored, calmed, and cared for throughout the entire flight. We send you a photo and update at departure, upon landing, and whenever possible during the flight.

6
Safe Delivery to Your Destination

Upon landing, your flight nanny delivers your pet to you at the airport or your door (door-to-door service available). Your pet arrives calm, healthy, and happy — right into your arms.

Eligibility

Is My Pet Eligible for In-Cabin Flight Travel?

In-cabin pet travel has specific size and weight requirements set by airlines. Here's a general guide — we'll confirm exact eligibility for your pet during consultation.

✅ Typically Eligible
  • 🐶 Small dogs under ~20 lbs (pet + carrier combined)
  • 🐱 Most domestic cats
  • 🐾 Toy and small breeds (Chihuahua, Maltese, Shih Tzu, etc.)
  • 🐕 Miniature & small Dachshunds, Pomeranians, Yorkies
⚠️ Requires Consultation
  • 🐶 Dogs near the weight limit
  • 😤 Brachycephalic breeds (Bulldogs, Pugs, Boston Terriers)
  • 📏 Pets requiring larger carriers
  • 💊 Pets with medical conditions or medications
